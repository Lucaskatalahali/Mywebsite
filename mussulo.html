Ilha do Mussulo (Mussulo Island)

Mussulo Island is one of the most cherished destinations near Luanda, Angola. Located just a short boat ride from the city, Mussulo offers a peaceful escape from the urban buzz. The island is actually a peninsula formed by ocean currents, creating calm waters perfect for swimming and relaxing. Mussulo is known for its beautiful sandy beaches, gentle waves, and palm-lined shores. Visitors often enjoy water sports, beach games, or simply lounging in the sun. Along the coast you'll find charming beach huts, cozy resorts, and local vendors selling seafood and refreshing drinks. Whether you're planning a quiet day in nature or a fun gathering with friends and family, Mussulo provides the ideal backdrop.
