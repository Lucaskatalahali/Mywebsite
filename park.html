The Wildlife Sanctuary of Angola


Quiçama National Park

Located about 70 km south of Luanda, Quiçama National Park is one of Angola's natural treasures. Covering an area of over 9,600 km², it is the largest conservation park in the country and home to an impressive variety of wildlife and flora. Beyond its fascinating wildlife, Quiçama offers breathtaking landscapes, ranging from open savannas to dense forests and the majestic Kwanza River. Visitors can explore the park through organized safaris, observing animals in their natural habitat and experiencing the untouched beauty of Angolan nature. If you're looking for an authentic wildlife experience, Quiçama National Park is the perfect destination!
